About Reed's Bitcoin Classroom

Bitcoin Classroom was started in January 2024 with a mission of bringing new skills to local Bitcoiners. Bitcoin Classroom wants to spread practical, hands-on Bitcoin knowledge to Bitcoiners who learn better in a classroom environment, as opposed to self-taught from YouTube or other online resources.

Our model is to provide computers, hardware wallets, or other physical devices and allow students to actually use them and set them up in a classroom setting. Students come to Bitcoin Classroom to learn and will never set up wallets that they will actually send actual Bitcoin to.

Our first two classes are a Multisig-101 and a Multisig-201 class. In the Multisig-101 class, even students with no prior Bitcoin knowledge can set up and use a 2-of-3 multisig wallet in Sparrow. In the Multisig-201 class, students will focus on restoring multisig wallets in a variety of ways, including using different software programs and different combinations of keys.

In the future, we will have a class on setting up a Start9 Node, and students will have the option of paying for the hardware and taking it home! We will also have a similar option for our Bitcoin mining class in the future.
